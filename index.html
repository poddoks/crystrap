Competitive Roblox Bootstrapper

Crystrap is built for players who care about frames, delay, and nothing else.

Crystrap is a performance-first Roblox bootstrapper based on Bloxstrap. It is tuned for cleaner launches, lower overhead, harder optimization, and aggressive visual cutbacks when pure responsiveness matters more than looks.

What It Does

Everything in Crystrap is pointed at smoother gameplay.

Performance Presets

Use bundled presets like Poddoks Fast Flags and Ultra Low Delay for immediate max-FPS, lower-delay tuning.

Optimization Controls

Control framerate, D3D11, VSync, telemetry, terrain textures, rendering load, mesh detail, and more.

Asset Trim Mode

Cut out large chunks of bundled Roblox assets like textures, models, sky, sounds, fonts, and extra content.

Repair And Cleanup

Repair broken Roblox installs before launch and clear stale Crystrap-managed versions and cache leftovers.

Manual NVIDIA Bundle

Optional NVIDIA Profile Inspector bundle for users who want to apply the full tweak manually, not through presets.

Auto Updating

Ship a new GitHub release and users can pull the update when they reopen the app.
